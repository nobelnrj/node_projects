It's like there is no world without SmartPhones

The yearly survey

This survey about the market value of smartphones starts from 2010 to 2019. The survey has some content which really astonishes. The whole of the year 2010 was dominated by Nokia. Nokia had 33.17% of the market whereas Apple had 30.19%. This may not seem to be a big difference but it's just the beginning. By the end of 2010 the top five companies were Nokia, Apple, RIM(Blackberry), Samsung and SonyEricsson. The next year (2011) was also dominated by Nokia which had 39.72% of the market whereas Apple had 26.15%. Now i hope you understand what I meant earlier. By the end of 2011 the top five companies were Nokia, Apple, Samsung, RIM(Blackberry) and SonyEricsson. Apple started dominating Nokia by the end of 2012. It was this when HTC registered its name in world histroy by eliminating RIM(Blackberry) out of top five companies. Apple finally achieved it's goal which didn't last much. By the end of 2013 Samsung dominated 27.7% of the market value with Apple having just 21.3%. Then started the real race between Samsung and Apple. But by the end of 2014 Samsung reached the level of Nokia by holding 33.34% of the market. At the same time LG became the forth in the market value. The year 2015 was not the year for Apple because the market value kept decreasing. By the end Samsung had 30.84% whereas Apple had 19.5%. The same continued the whole of 2016. This is when Huawei became forth which resulted in Nokia being fifth in market value. The year 2017 was also the same but with the sad part in smartphone history. Nokia was completely removed from top eight positions by new companies like Xiaomi ,OPPO and Motorola. Even though Apple was not able to defeated Samsung in market the release of "iPhone X" was a significant one. Till 2019 Samsung remains the top most company in world market with an potential opponent (Huawei) in third position.

New invention by every companies

Though most of the companies does not look good when a survey of their market value is taken into account. But all the smartphone companies that exist have specialized themselves in certain fields. For example Apple is the first company to integrote finger print scanner in smartphones. Samsung is the first company to implement curved screen to provide best viewing angles for the user. Nokia as we all know is known for it's build quality. RIM(Blackberry) is the most secured phone which many people prefer to buy. Huawei and OPPO are known for their camera. They developed their camera ability in such a way that it replaced DSLR. Oneplus which till now didn't achieve big things it smartphone market is the first smartphone company to provide Amoled display on mid-range smartphones. Xiaomi provided the cheaper phones with high performance. Then came new inventions like notch and pop-up cameras which was adopted by all companies. So from the above content it should be clear for you that no company stayed in the market without creating something new. People always want something new for their eyes. First smartphones were with big bezels and small screen. Then phones with notch to replace those bezel and make the view port bigger. Even then the developers were not satisfied. They wanted the users to experience more which lead to removing the notch and bringing in dotted notch or pop-up cameras to enable full screen display for the users.
